Journey to Stardom

Mike Drucker’s journey in the world of comedy and television is nothing short of inspiring. Born on March 15, 1984, in the United States, Mike’s early passion for comedy was evident.

Building a Career in Comedy Writing

Mike’s entry into television writing began with his role at Up at Noon with Greg Miller in 2012, which marked his transition from stand-up comedy to a structured writing environment.

His work caught the eye of industry heavyweights, leading to a significant position as a writer for The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon in New York City.
