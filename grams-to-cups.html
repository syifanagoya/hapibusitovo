Convert grams to cups – grams into cups

To get the exact conversion of grams to cups, you will need to know the density of your particular ingredient. A quick online search should give you the answer if you don’t know it.

cups = grams/236.59 × ingredient density

Therefore, to convert grams to cups, simply divide the weight of the grams by the result of (236.59 x ingredient density). Doing this will give you the equivalent weight in cups for your recipe.

For example, if you have 240 grams of water, dividing it by 236.59 would give you a result is 240 grams to cups equivalent 1.0144 cups of water.

Grams to cups nz

In New Zealand, the metric system is used for measuring ingredients when cooking. Therefore, when a recipe calls for grams, it can be necessary to convert the measurement into cups. To convert grams to cups nz, divide the weight in grams by the number 250 (the number of grams in a cup).

Cups of nz = grams/250 × ingredient density

Grams to cups uk

In the United Kingdom, recipes may use metric measurements or imperial measures. Since the UK uses Imperial sizes for baking and cooking, it is necessary to convert grams into cups when a recipe calls for grams.

Cups of uk = grams/250 × ingredient density

To convert Grams to cups UK, divide the weight in grams by the number 250 (the number of grams in a metric cup).
