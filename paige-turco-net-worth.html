Early Life and Career Beginnings

Paige Turco, born on May 17, 1965, in Boston, Massachusetts, had a rich childhood filled with aspirations of becoming a classical ballerina.

She took ballet lessons and performed with the New England Dance Conservatory, Amherst Ballet Theatre Company, and Western Massachusetts Ballet Company.

Unfortunately, an ankle injury ended her ballet dreams, leading her to pursue a career in acting.

Turco attended the Walnut Hill School and later graduated from Bay Path College, majoring in drama at the University of Connecticut.

Her acting debut came in 1987 on the CBS soap opera Guiding Light as Dinah Marler, quickly followed by a role on All My Children as Melanie Cortlandt.
