What Can We Anticipate from Magnum PI Season 5?

A remake of the popular 1980s crime drama with the same name, Magnum P.I. stars Hernandez as Thomas Magnum, an ex-Navy SEAL who has transitioned into a slick-talking private investigator, according to NBC Insider.

When Magnum isn’t providing security advice to the enigmatic author Robin Masters, he and his devoted squad take on some of the craziest situations Hawaii has ever encountered. Picking up where Season 4 left off, Season 5 will surely have a bevy of adventures in Hawaii.

The fourth season’s concluding episode, “Close to Home,” showed viewers that Gordon’s ex-wife Beth had been kidnapped, and the kidnappers had demanded that Gordon kill his ex-partner Whelan, who was being held captive.

After realizing that the blackmailer was Ray Nasano, who killed Gordon’s sister Kim, Magnum and Higgins help Gordon free Whelan from custody.  Beth is kept alive and Ray is apprehended, but Gordon is let go by the HPD. Who Will Return in Magnum PI Season 5?

Michael Rady has been cast in a recurring role as HPD Detective Chris Childs, beginning in the season premiere, according to a report from TVLine on October 3, 2022. The following is a list of the series’ primary cast members and a description of each character:

  • Jay Hernandez as Thomas Magnum: A former Navy SEAL who is a security consultant for the successful novelist Robin Masters, living in the guest house on his estate and working as a private investigator.
  • Perdita Weeks as Juliet Higgins: A former MI6 agent who is majordomo to Robin Masters; she and Magnum bicker but become allies.
  • Zachary Knighton as Orville “Rick” Wright: A Marine veteran and former door gunner who runs his tiki bar and is also a playboy
  • Stephen Hill as Theodore “T.C.” Calvin: A Marine veteran and helicopter pilot who runs helicopter tours of Hawaii and is a member of Magnum’s team
  • Amy Hill as Teuila “Kumu” Tuileta: The cultural curator of Robin Masters’ estate
