What happened to Brittany Stykes?

On Wednesday, August 28, 2013, Brittany Stykes participated in an electronic job interview.

When the online interview ended, she was optimistic it had gone well. 

After having a meal with her mother-in-law, she announced she would drop in on her parents later that evening.

At about 7:00 p.m., Brittany left her in-laws’ house with a plate laden with Shawn’s favorite leftovers. 

She buckled her daughter Aubree into the car seat.

Brittany entered the driver’s seat of the family’s yellow Jeep Wrangler and set off toward Ripley.

Her father’s 49th birthday was that day. She visited him and wished him well before returning to her husband.

Unfortunately, she never got to see her husband.

Who discovered Brittany Stykes body?

Just after 8:00, a man traveling down US 68 was astonished to see headlights emerging from the woods on the other side of the road.

Worried, he turned around and stopped to look into the situation.

He stumbled upon a yellow Jeep whose engine was still functioning.

Shockingly, he saw a woman inside injured in a vehicle crash from the amount of blood she was coated in. 

Her lips were bluish and she was slumped over the wheel. It was obvious she was not breathing.

In his call to 911, the man stated, “There’s a Jeep in the woods. Someone seemed to have fallen asleep while driving and crashed into the woods.”

As he waited for help, the man saw a toddler in the Jeep’s passenger seat.

The little girl was covered in blood but was awake and calling out for her mom.

Unbeknownst to him then, he had just stumbled across Brittany and Aubree.

Both Brittany and Aubree had been shot. So it was clear they were not dealing with a traffic accident. 

Brittany was dead on the spot and sadly, nothing could have been done to save her.

Both she and the baby she was carrying passed away. 

A medical team desperately worked to save Aubree’s life at Cincinnati Children’s Hospital.

She was rushed there by ambulance.

The five bullet holes in the Jeep’s driver-side door indicated someone fired from a distance. 

Two bullets struck Brittany. The first pierced her neck and the second hit her chest and right arm. 

The bullet that hit Aubree went through her forehead and out the top of her skull. After two quick operations, her condition miraculously improved, and her survival was predicted.

The bystander, police, and Shane discovered Brittany’s body in her Jeep.

Brittany Stykes: Who killed her?
